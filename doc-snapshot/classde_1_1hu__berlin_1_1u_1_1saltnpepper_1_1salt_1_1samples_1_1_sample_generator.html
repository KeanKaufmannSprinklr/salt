Creates a SDocumentGraph containing to texts of two different speakers, who are aligned via the STimeline related to the SToken objects.

The texts are { PRIMARY_TEXT_EN_SPK1} and { PRIMARY_TEXT_EN_SPK2}, which are tokenized by words. The words 'to' and 'Oh' have been said simultaneously and are overlapping via the timeline.
